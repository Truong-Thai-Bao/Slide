3. Feelings & States (Cảm xúc & Trạng thái)

Stative Verbs: Love, Like, Hate, Know, Understand, Need, Want.

(Động từ chỉ trạng thái -> KHÔNG dùng V-ing)

Wrong

Right

I like you.(Tôi thích bạn)

She knows him.(Cô ấy biết anh ấy)

7/25

Part 1: The Verb "TO BE"

To Be = Thì, Là, Ở

  • I → Am
  • He, She, It → Is
  • You, We, They → Are

Ex: I am happy.(Tôi cảm thấy hạnh phúc)

Ex: She is a doctor.(Cô ấy là bác sĩ)

Ex: They are at home.(Họ đang ở nhà)

8/25

WARNING: Be vs. Action (Cảnh báo quan trọng!)

Do not mix TO BE with ACTION VERBS.

(Đừng trộn lẫn To Be và Động từ thường)

Wrong

I am go to school.

He is play football.

Right

I go to school.(Tôi đi học)

He plays football.(Anh ấy chơi bóng đá)

Consonant + Y

Study, Fly, Cry

(Phụ âm + Y → Đổi thành IES)

16/25

Negative Form (-)

DON'T

I / You / We / They

"I don't like fish."

"(Tôi không thích cá)"

DOESN'T

He / She / It

"She doesn't like apples."

"(Cô ấy không thích táo)"

17/25

Important Note! (Lưu ý quan trọng)

When you use DOESN'T, the verb has NO 'S'.

(Khi đã dùng Doesn't, động từ KHÔNG thêm S nữa)

Wrong

She doesn't likes tea.

Right

She doesn't like tea.

(Cô ấy không thích trà)

(Because the Superman S moved to Doesn't)

18/25

Yes / No Questions (?)

Do + I / You / We / They + Verb?

→ Yes, I do. / No, I don't.

Example: Do you work here?


Does + He / She / It + Verb?

→ Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.

Example: Does she work here?

(Never use ARE with verbs!
"Are you go?" is wrong.)

19/25

Wh- Questions (Câu hỏi có từ để hỏi)

(Wh-) + Do/Does + Subject + Verb?

Where

Where do you live?

(Bạn sống ở đâu?)

What

What does she eat?

(Cô ấy ăn gì?)

When

When do they sleep?

(Họ ngủ khi nào?)

20/25

Signal Words (Dấu hiệu)

Always - Usually - Often - Sometimes - Never

Position (Vị trí)

1. Before Action Verb: S + always + V + (O)

Example: I always run.(Tôi luôn chạy)

2. After To Be:S + to be + always + V + (O)

Example: She is always late.(Cô ấy luôn trễ)
